Baked Potato

back to Homepage

Description

The perfect and simple baked potato recipe with a crispy outside, a soft fluffy inside, and an overall delicious dish!

Preparation: 10 mins   Cook Time: 40 mins   Total Time: 1 hr   Servings: 1 potato per person

Ingredients

  • Russet potatoes
  • Butter or Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Freshly-Ground Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Pre-Heat oven to 450°F
  2. Prepare your backing sheet for the potatoes to sit on
  3. Poke the potatoes to prevent losing potatoes whiles it is being baked
  4. Bake the potatoes in the oven for about 25 minutes
  5. Take out the potatoes to brush them with butter/oil
  6. Put the potates back in the oven for about 20 more minutes until the insides are soft
  7. Serve with pepper, salt, and any more seasoning of your choice!
