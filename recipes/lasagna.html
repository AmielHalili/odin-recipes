Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe may a little tedious, but it is so tasty that it will be worth it!

Preparation: 30 mins   Cook Time: 2 hrs 30 mins   Total Time: 3 hrs 15 mins   Servings: 12

Ingredients

  • Italian Sausage
  • Ground Beef
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • 1 Can of Crushed Tomatoes
  • 2 Cans of Tomato Sauce
  • 2 Cans of Tomato Paste
  • Seasoning and Spices (Parsley, Dried Basil Leaves, Salt, Italian Seasoning, Fennel Seeds, Black Pepper)
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese (Parmesan, Mozzarella, Ricotta)
  • 1 Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce with the italian sausage and ground beef
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture and have cheese ready
  4. Layer the lasagna in the following order:
    1. Meat Sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta Mixture
    4. Mozzarella Slices
    5. Meat Sauce
    6. Parmesan Cheese
    7. Repeat until near the top
    8. Finish the top with parmesan
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest for about 15 minutes before serving
